All About Poppy

Here you will find a photo gallery of Poppy, a 5-year-old American shorthair tortoiseshell cat. She was found abandoned by her mother when she was only a few days old. My family adopted her from a foster home a few months later. Poppy enjoys roaming the outdoors, catching mice, and knocking things over. Click around to find photos, fun facts, and more!

Thanks for visiting Poppy's website and be sure to check back later for more photos and updates.
